Gross descriptionEntered by Dr. R. Eshcol · 04-14 17:22

Specimen A — Received fresh, labeled "right breast partial mastectomy, short stitch superior, long stitch lateral", consisting of an oriented portion of fibrofatty breast tissue measuring 6.4 × 5.1 × 3.0 cm and weighing 72 g. The specimen is inked: anterior — blue, posterior — black, superior — green, inferior — yellow, medial — red, lateral — orange. Serial sectioning reveals a firm, gritty, white-tan, stellate mass measuring 2.4 × 1.9 × 1.6 cm, located 4 mm from the closest (posterior) margin. The remaining parenchyma shows yellow-tan adipose tissue with focal areas of fibrosis. Representative sections in cassettes A1–A14.

Specimen B — Received in formalin, labeled "left axillary sentinel nodes", consisting of four (4) tan-pink lymph nodes, ranging from 0.6 to 1.4 cm. Each is bisected and submitted entirely; B1 = node 1 (1.4 cm, gross unremarkable); B2-B4 likewise.

Specimen C — Received in formalin, labeled "cavity shaves", consisting of six (6) separately oriented and inked tissue fragments, each ranging from 0.5–1.2 cm in greatest dimension. Each is submitted entirely; C1=anterior, C2=posterior, C3=superior, C4=inferior, C5=medial, C6=lateral.

Microscopic description

A1–A8 (tumor) — Sections show an invasive ductal carcinoma arranged in nests, cords, and tubules, infiltrating fibrous stroma. Tubule formation in <10% of the tumor (score 3). Nuclei are mildly enlarged with moderate pleomorphism (score 2). Mitotic count is 8 mitoses per 10 HPF (score 2). Nottingham grade 2 of 3 (score 7). Foci of lymphovascular invasion are identified at the periphery, > 3 foci. No definite perineural invasion. Adjacent ductal carcinoma in situ, low-intermediate grade, cribriform pattern, ~5% of tumor volume; no necrosis, no microcalcifications in DCIS.

A9–A14 (margins, normal parenchyma) — All inked margins are negative for invasive carcinoma and DCIS. Closest margin is posterior at 4 mm. Background breast shows usual ductal hyperplasia and columnar cell change.

B1 (SLN-1) — One focus of tumor cells measuring 1.8 mm in greatest dimension within the subcapsular sinus, confirmed on cytokeratin (AE1/AE3) immunostain. Consistent with micrometastasis (pN1mi). No extranodal extension. B2–B4 — negative for metastatic carcinoma on H&E and CK; isolated tumor cells not identified.

C1–C6 — All shave margins negative for invasive carcinoma and DCIS.
